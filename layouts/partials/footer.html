Dirty Ol'Ambusher is 1/3 of the post-avant-post-rock trio Ambusher. Plowing headlong into the outer limits of good taste with his electronic guitar (aka, Summoner of Satan), and often leaving the audience far behind, "The Dirtster" creates an experience like no other. Perplexed. Overjoyed. Angered. Ecstatic. Disappointed. Serene. These are all common, usually simultaneous, reactions to encountering the "far out" sounds of "El Dirterido" for the first time.
